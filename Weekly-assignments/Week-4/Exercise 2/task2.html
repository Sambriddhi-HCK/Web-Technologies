Subway Surfers


Image

Subway Surfers is a single player mobile gaming app and website developed by Kiloo. The game sarts with the player graffitiing a wall of the trainstation. The aim of the game is to outrun a train inspector while dodging upcoming trains and objects. Collecting coins and items on the way gives the player advantages like long jumps and magnets to collect more coins. I loved playing this game when I was a child. Although it has been a long time since I last played this game. When the assignment was to mention oneo of my favorite games, Subway Surfers came first to my mind. It is a never ending game as long as the objects can be dodged.
